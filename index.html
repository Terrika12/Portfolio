About Me

Front-End Developer - Web Designer - Interaction Designer - Computer Programmer

Hello & welcome to my page! My name is Terrika(pronounced like Erica but with a "T"). I grew up in a very small town known as Utica, MS. Family has always been an important part of my life. Even until this day, I strive to make them proud with everything I do. Some of my hobbies include reading, exercising, cooking, singing, drawing, and playing video games. I attended Mississippi State University and obtained my Bachelor of Biological Science degree in 2017. I have always been intrigued by different forms of art. I am also always looking for ways to challenge myself. Coding has definitely not been a walk in the park and is a way for me to express my creativity, so it's a perfect fit. I first began learning how to code in the beginning of August 2021. All thanks goes to MS Coding Academy for the opportunity. I am currently still learning and have a lot more to learn. What I enjoy most about coding is that there are multiple ways to do so. I am looking forward to learn more ways to put my creativity to use.
